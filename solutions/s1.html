S1. Community Device Libraries

Narrow device gaps quickly with shared, maintained laptops/tablets.

Steps to Implement

  • Collect/refurbish devices; barcode tracking
  • Set loan policy & support hours
  • Basic repair station
  • Usage dashboard (manual logs)

Impact & SDG Alignment

Increases equitable access; supports homework and AI-assisted practice.

Risks & Mitigation

Device loss/damage → deposits, spare parts; fair access rules.
