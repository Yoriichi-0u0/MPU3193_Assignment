Lecture Connections

Epistemology, Ethics, Logic, Human Being, and Decolonization.

Why Philosophy Matters Here

Equitable education is not only technical; it is ethical and epistemic. We link our cases and solutions to five lecture themes.

Epistemology

Access conditions who can know; digital exclusion is an epistemic injustice.

Ethics

AI should reduce inequality; disclosure and consent matter.

Logic

Avoid the fallacy that “access = learning.” Evidence is required.

Concept of Human Being

Education grows agency and dignity; AI assists, teachers lead.

Decolonization

Co-create local content and language support.
