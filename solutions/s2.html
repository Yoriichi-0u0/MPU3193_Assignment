S2. Offline-First Learning Packs

Resilient learning when bandwidth drops.

Steps to Implement

  • Design printable core sheets
  • Create lightweight HTML on USB/SD
  • Schedule weekly sync windows

Impact & SDG Alignment

Keeps learning going during outages; inclusive for low-bandwidth homes.

Risks & Mitigation

Version drift → clear dates on packs; simple checklists.
