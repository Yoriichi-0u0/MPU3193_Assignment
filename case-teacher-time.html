Case Study

Freeing Time: AI for Teacher Feedback and Prep

Summary

AI can support teachers by drafting outlines, checking alignment to outcomes and generating practice questions—not to replace teachers but to free hours for mentoring. Quality rises when teachers spend time where it matters most: feedback, discussion and care.

Factors

Burnout and limited time constrain personalised feedback. Schools weigh PD investment and procurement. Digital workflows reduce printing and travel. Workload agreements, IP and data governance influence adoption.

Implications

Better feedback deepens learning and engagement. Improved retention and outcomes are possible. Shareable prompt banks and exemplars can spread effective practice.

Solutions

Provide PD on prompt craft, evaluation and bias awareness. Blend AI‑drafted materials with teacher voice and local context. Time‑box AI planning to ensure purpose, not dependence.

Connection to Lecture Topics

Epistemology foregrounds human judgement; Logic supports error spotting; Concept of Human Being prioritises mentorship and care.
